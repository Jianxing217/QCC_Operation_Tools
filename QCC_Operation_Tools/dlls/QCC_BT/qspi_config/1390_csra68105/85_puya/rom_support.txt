MIB keys are required to support Puya parts on CSRA68105 devices. There are
no Puya QSPI parts with ROM support on CSRA68105.


The following QSPI parts can not be supported at all due to issues with erase/program
suspend/resume implementation:

Puya P25Q80L
Puya P25Q80LE
Puya P25Q16L
Puya P25Q16LE
